\section{Power Series}
Recall $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}{c_n{x^{n}}}$ has a radius of convergence $R=\frac{1}{\limsup_{n\to \infty}{\sqrtn{\left|c_{n}\right| }}}$ such that the series absolutely converges for $\left|x\right| < R$, diverges for $\left|x\right| >R$ and anything possible for $\left|x\right| =R$.
